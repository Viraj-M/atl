neptune

It takes Neptune 164.8 Earth years to orbit the Sun. The orbit path of Neptune is approximately 30 astronomical units (AU) from the Sun. Neptune has the second largest gravity of any planet in the solar system – second only to Jupiter. Neptune is the Roman God of the Sea. Neptune was discovered by Jean Joseph Le Verrier.
Neptune spins on its axis very rapidly. Neptune is the smallest of the ice giants. The atmosphere of Neptune is made of hydrogen and helium, with some methane. Neptune has 14 moons. Neptune was not known to the ancients. Neptune has a very thin collection of rings. Neptune has a very active climate.
Neptune is a gaseous planet, composed of hydrogen, helium, methane, with traces of ammonia and water. Neptune was discovered by Urbain Le Verrier, John Couch Adams, and Johann Galle on September 23, 1846. Neptune is the fourth largest planet in the Solar System.
